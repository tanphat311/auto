1.\tVoltage Transformation: The ignition coil transforms the low voltage from the battery to the high voltage required to create a spark.\n2.\tSpark Timing: The control module or distributor ensures that the spark occurs at the right time for each cylinder.\n3.\tSpark Distribution: The distributor or control module sends the high-voltage spark to the correct spark plug.\n4.\tIgnition: The spark plugs ignite the air-fuel mixture, causing combustion and powering the engine.\n
